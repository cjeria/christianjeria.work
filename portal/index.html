Branding 2025

Portal

Bringing early product concepts to life through rapid prototyping, illustration, and design exploration.

Overview

At PortalHQ, I partnered with the team to bring early product concepts to life through rapid prototyping and design exploration. My contributions spanned across product, marketing, and brand presence: helping the company validate ideas for wallet services, shaping product illustrations, supporting marketing campaigns, and fully maintaining and evolving their website.

The Problem

PortalHQ needed a way to communicate and test their vision for wallet services with both users and potential partners. As an early-stage startup, they required prototypes to validate concepts quickly, clear marketing to grow awareness, and a functional website to establish credibility.

The Solution

I designed and prototyped wallet service concepts to help the team explore and test product directions. Alongside this, I supported their go-to-market efforts with cohesive product illustrations, visual storytelling, and hands-on marketing design. To complete the foundation, I fully maintained and iterated on their website, ensuring it reflected the company's evolving identity and product vision.

The Impact

My work enabled PortalHQ to test product ideas faster, present their vision more clearly to users and investors, and build a credible online presence. By combining product design, marketing support, and web maintenance, I helped the team move quickly from concept to communication, strengthening their ability to grow and position themselves in the competitive wallet services space.
